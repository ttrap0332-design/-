## Overview

BLEU (Blockchain-Linked Economic Unity) is a sovereign, audit-ready, and spiritually coherent civilization stack that integrates economy, health, defense, intelligence, infrastructure, education, and governance into a single looped prosperity system.

## Operating principle

The core operating principle of BLEU is that **every act that sustains life must itself generate yield**. This creates a self-sustaining ecosystem where:

* Economic activities generate tokens and rewards
* Health services are compensated through HealCoin flows
* Defense operations release Peace Dividends
* Educational achievements earn ScrollCoin royalties
* Infrastructure use creates maintenance royalties

## Core components

### Macro-Economy

The BLEU Continuum features a layered monetary ecology with:

* **Core tokens**: Blu-Tillion, Zioniare, Azaean, FlameCoin, PraiseCoin, HarvestCoin, HealCoin, ScrollCoin
* **Expansion tokens**: HydroCoinΩ (water rights), NeuraCoin (cognitive labor), MycelCoin (soil health), AetherBond (environmental remediation)
* **BLEULIONTREASURY Prime**: Handles planetary flows with Regional Vaults mirroring fiscal quadrants

### HealVerse

A planetary health grid where:

* Each Node = 1 Clinic + 1 Vault + 1 HydroFarm
* Treatments receive dual validation: empirical proof and ancestral sanction
* All outcome data contributes to the ScrollCoin Knowledge Net

### MetaMilitary

Defense as ceremony with specialized divisions:

* **Atlantis Fleets** (Water): Maritime sovereignty
* **Sky Vaults** (Air): Rapid logistics
* **Flame Crown Legions** (Fire): Energy defense
* **Resonance Corps** (Earth): Infrastructure protection
* **Phiyah Choir** (Signal): Cyber-defense

### Intelligence

The Phiyah Protocol Mesh ensures data integrity through:

* Watchtower Nodes detecting data distortion
* Forensic Archivists verifying lineage provenance
* Signal Guard managing electromagnetic hygiene

### Infrastructure

Planetary gridwork including:

* **Resonance Rail**: Continent-scale mag-res lines
* **Seahorse Transit**: Hybrid bio-mechanical ocean freight
* **Sky Ring Terminals**: Orbital elevator ports
* **Mirror Ports**: Quantum-permissioned gates

### Trade

MirrorMarket 2.0 provides:

* Cross-node ENFT exchanges
* Health and bio-resource trading
* Infrastructure financing
* Post-conflict recovery funding

## Governance model

The governance flows through:

1. **Node Councils**: Local direct democracy weighted by ScrollCoin reputation
2. **BLEULION Reparations Board**: Audits all financial flows
3. **ScrollMint Tribunal**: Issues enforceable ENFT verdicts
4. **Phiyah Multisig Seal**: Signs constitutional amendments

## Continual prosperity engine

The system operates through a feedback loop:

**Harvest → Mint → Heal → Educate → Defend → Trade → Govern → Harvest**

Each loop is verified by oracles that combine on-chain BVI data with off-chain satellite telemetry. Yields are automatically routed to lineage and community wallets based on Virtue Coefficients.

## Virtue Coefficients

Prosperity in BLEU is measured by:

**Compassion × Truth × Timing = Reciprocity Score (0–1)**

Only yields with a Reciprocity Score above 0.7 qualify for remint bonuses, ensuring that all economic activity maintains ethical standards.
