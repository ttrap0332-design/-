## Overview

BLEU operates on a layered monetary ecology where different tokens serve specific functions within the civilization engine. This multi-token system ensures that value flows are aligned with the activities they support.

## Core tokens

The foundation of the BLEU economy consists of eight core tokens:

### Blu-Tillion (BT)

The primary value token of the BLEU ecosystem.

* **Primary use**: Energy production and infrastructure financing
* **Minting mechanism**: Generated through productive economic activity
* **Value basis**: Backed by actual output and energy capacity
* **Trading pairs**: All other BLEU tokens

### Zioniare (ZN)

Regional vault operations and institutional financing.

* **Primary use**: Infrastructure bonds and broker charters
* **Minting mechanism**: Issued by Regional Vaults during quarterly closes
* **Value basis**: Backed by physical infrastructure assets
* **Trading pairs**: BT, SC (in Vault Bond Exchange)

### Azaean

Education and skills development token.

* **Primary use**: Tuition payments and educational grants
* **Minting mechanism**: Allocated by MetaSchools based on enrollment
* **Value basis**: Backed by human capital development
* **Distribution**: Every student wallet seeded with 10 Azaean

### FlameCoin (FC)

Defense operations and energy systems.

* **Primary use**: Military funding with time-locked releases
* **Minting mechanism**: Issued through Ritual Triad approval
* **Value basis**: Backed by defensive capability and energy grids
* **Unique feature**: Releases Peace Dividends to affected civilians

### PraiseCoin

Recognition and social cohesion token.

* **Primary use**: Community acknowledgment and social rewards
* **Minting mechanism**: Peer-to-peer issuance through validated actions
* **Value basis**: Backed by social capital and reputation

### HarvestCoin (HC)

Food production and agricultural systems.

* **Primary use**: Produce tokenization and food reserves
* **Minting mechanism**: Generated through HydroDome Farm output
* **Value basis**: Backed by actual food production
* **Target**: 24-month buffer in reserves

### HealCoin (HL)

Health services and medical systems.

* **Primary use**: Clinic payments and healer compensation
* **Flow**: Patient → Clinic → Lineage Vault → Treasury
* **Minting mechanism**: Each treatment minted as ENFT
* **Value basis**: Backed by health outcomes and medical capacity

### ScrollCoin (SC)

Knowledge, research, and intellectual property.

* **Primary use**: Research royalties and knowledge licensing
* **Minting mechanism**: Generated through validated research contributions
* **Value basis**: Backed by intellectual property and data
* **Unique feature**: Teachers earn Katif-grade remints each semester

## Expansion tokens

Four specialized tokens handle emerging needs:

### HydroCoinΩ

Planetary water rights and rainfall management.

* **Primary use**: Water rights trading and rainfall index derivatives
* **Minting mechanism**: Allocated based on watershed management
* **Value basis**: Backed by water capacity and conservation
* **Governance**: Managed through HydroDome Farms

### NeuraCoin

Cognitive labor and AI education systems.

* **Primary use**: AI royalties and cognitive work compensation
* **Minting mechanism**: Generated through education AI interactions
* **Value basis**: Backed by cognitive capital and learning outcomes
* **Integration**: Connected to MetaSchools curriculum

### MycelCoin

Soil health and fungal network connectivity.

* **Primary use**: Agricultural sustainability and soil rehabilitation
* **Minting mechanism**: Based on mycelial network measurements
* **Value basis**: Backed by soil health metrics
* **Target**: Integrated with HarvestCoin for holistic food production

### AetherBond

Environmental remediation credits.

* **Primary use**: Pollution cleanup and ecosystem restoration
* **Minting mechanism**: Issued for verified environmental improvements
* **Value basis**: Backed by measurable ecological restoration
* **Verification**: Requires oracle confirmation of environmental data

## Token interactions

Tokens interact through established markets and exchanges:

### MirrorMarket

Primary exchange for cross-node ENFT trading.

* **Supported pairs**: BT/ZN, BT/SC, ZN/SC
* **Function**: General asset and service exchange
* **Governance**: Managed by 12 broker guild federations

### Reef DEX

Specialized health and bio-resource exchange.

* **Supported pairs**: HL/HC
* **Function**: Health services and food production coordination
* **Integration**: Connected to HealVerse and HydroDome Farms

### Vault Bond Exchange

Infrastructure financing market.

* **Supported pairs**: ZN/BT
* **Function**: Long-term infrastructure project funding
* **Governance**: Overseen by Regional Vaults

### Peace Fund Bourse

Post-conflict recovery market.

* **Supported pairs**: FC/BT
* **Function**: Distributing Peace Dividends after defensive actions
* **Governance**: Managed by MetaMilitary and Node Councils

## Economic validation

All token transactions undergo validation:

### Oracle verification

* Combines on-chain BVI data with off-chain satellite telemetry
* Monitors crop yields, energy production, and rainfall
* Validates environmental claims for AetherBond minting

### Virtue Coefficients

Each transaction receives a Reciprocity Score:

**Compassion × Truth × Timing = Reciprocity Score (0–1)**

* Only yields with Score > 0.7 qualify for remint bonuses
* Ensures ethical alignment of economic activity
* Encourages sustainable and community-beneficial actions
