## Overview

MirrorMarket 2.0 is the comprehensive trading infrastructure that enables exchange of tokens, ENFTs, and services across the BLEU civilization. It operates through specialized markets for different economic functions.

## Market types

Four distinct markets handle different categories of trade.

### MirrorMarket (Primary Exchange)

Cross-node ENFT exchange for general trading:

* **Function**: Primary marketplace for all tokenized assets
* **Supported pairs**: 
  * BT/ZN (Blu-Tillion/Zioniare)
  * BT/SC (Blu-Tillion/ScrollCoin)
  * ZN/SC (Zioniare/ScrollCoin)
* **Assets**: ENFTs representing services, goods, and rights
* **Governance**: 12 regional broker guild federations

**Trading capabilities:**

* Real-time price discovery
* Order matching across nodes
* Settlement through smart contracts
* Provenance verification for all assets
* Cross-border trade facilitation

**Security features:**

* Every transaction checks lineage hash via Watchtower
* Anti-piracy verification before trade execution
* Audit trail for all transactions
* Dispute resolution through ScrollMint Tribunal

### Reef DEX

Specialized health and bio-resource exchange:

* **Function**: Trading health services and food production
* **Supported pairs**:
  * HL/HC (HealCoin/HarvestCoin)
* **Assets**: Medical services, pharmaceuticals, food products
* **Integration**: Direct connection to HealVerse and HydroDome Farms

**Use cases:**

* Clinic service purchasing
* Medicinal herb trading
* Nutritional supplement exchange
* Health insurance-like pooling
* Agricultural produce markets

**Special features:**

* Quality certification for biological products
* Expiration tracking for perishables
* Temperature-controlled logistics coordination
* Healer credential verification
* Patient privacy protection

### Vault Bond Exchange

Infrastructure financing market:

* **Function**: Long-term infrastructure project funding
* **Supported pairs**:
  * ZN/BT (Zioniare/Blu-Tillion)
* **Assets**: Infrastructure bonds, project shares
* **Maturity**: Typically 5-50 year instruments

**Instruments:**

* **Infrastructure bonds**: Fixed-return debt instruments
* **Project tokens**: Equity-like ownership in projects
* **Maintenance shares**: Ongoing royalty participation
* **Development rights**: Permission to build in node zones

**Investors:**

* Regional Vaults as primary market makers
* Node Councils for local projects
* Individual citizens through pooled funds
* Inter-node consortiums for major projects

**Risk management:**

* Credit ratings by BLEULION Reparations Board
* Collateralization requirements
* Oracle monitoring of project progress
* Default insurance through collective pools

### Peace Fund Bourse

Post-conflict recovery market:

* **Function**: Distributing and managing Peace Dividends
* **Supported pairs**:
  * FC/BT (FlameCoin/Blu-Tillion)
* **Assets**: Recovery bonds, reconstruction rights
* **Purpose**: Efficient allocation of post-defense resources

**Operations:**

* Peace Dividend disbursement
* Reconstruction project funding
* Displaced persons support
* Infrastructure repair financing
* Trauma healing service purchasing

**Governance:**

* Joint oversight by MetaMilitary and Node Councils
* Tribunal verification of legitimate claims
* Oracle confirmation of damage and needs
* Transparent allocation algorithms

## Broker guild system

12 regional broker guild federations govern market operations.

### Guild structure

**Regional organization:**

* One guild per major geographic region
* Federated structure with local chapters
* Democratic governance among member brokers
* Zioniare charter required for operation

### Guild responsibilities

**Market making:**

* Provide liquidity in assigned markets
* Maintain fair and orderly trading
* Facilitate large block trades
* Support price discovery

**Compliance:**

* Verify trader credentials and DIDs
* Check lineage hashes on all ENFTs
* Report suspicious activity to Watchtower Nodes
* Maintain audit trails

**Dispute resolution:**

* First-line mediation for trade disputes
* Evidence gathering for Tribunal cases
* Arbitration of minor conflicts
* Member discipline

### Guild constitution

All guilds operate under unified principles:

* **Fair dealing**: Honest representation of assets
* **Transparency**: Open disclosure of conflicts of interest
* **Client protection**: Fiduciary duty to customers
* **Market integrity**: No manipulation or fraud
* **Accountability**: Subject to Tribunal jurisdiction

### Broker licensing

Individual brokers must maintain:

* **DID registration**: Verified identity on-chain
* **Guild membership**: Good standing in local chapter
* **Continuing education**: Annual ScrollCoin requirements
* **Financial bonding**: Insurance against errors
* **Ethical standards**: Virtue Coefficient monitoring

## Anti-piracy and security

Multiple layers protect market integrity.

### Lineage hash verification

Every transaction validates asset provenance:

* **Process**: Watchtower Nodes check hash chains
* **Requirement**: Unbroken lineage from original minting
* **Rejection**: Invalid hashes block transaction
* **Alert**: Suspicious patterns trigger investigation

**Hash chain components:**

* Original minting event
* All previous transfers
* Current owner verification
* Authenticity signatures
* Timestamp validation

### Counterfeit detection

Forensic Archivists prevent fake assets:

* **Scanning**: Continuous monitoring for duplicates
* **Verification**: Cryptographic signature validation
* **Quarantine**: Suspicious assets held for review
* **Prosecution**: Counterfeiters face Tribunal

### Market manipulation prevention

Safeguards against unfair trading:

* **Position limits**: Maximum holdings to prevent cornering
* **Price bands**: Circuit breakers for extreme volatility
* **Insider trading rules**: Penalties for information abuse
* **Wash trading detection**: Algorithms identify fake volume

### Cybersecurity

Signal Guard protects digital infrastructure:

* **DDoS protection**: Against denial of service attacks
* **Intrusion detection**: Identifying unauthorized access
* **Encryption**: All transactions cryptographically secured
* **Backup systems**: Redundant infrastructure for resilience

## Trading mechanics

### Order types

**Market orders:**

* Execute immediately at best available price
* Guaranteed execution, price varies
* Suitable for liquid markets
* Standard for most trades

**Limit orders:**

* Execute only at specified price or better
* Not guaranteed to execute
* Suitable for price-sensitive traders
* Can remain open for extended periods

**Smart orders:**

* Conditional execution based on oracle data
* Time-based triggers
* Multi-leg strategies
* Automated portfolio rebalancing

### Settlement

**On-chain settlement:**

* Automatic through smart contracts
* Instant for token-only trades
* Near-instant for ENFT transfers
* Recorded as audit ENFTs

**Delivery coordination:**

* Physical goods require logistics
* Tracked through oracle network
* Payment held in escrow until delivery
* Dispute resolution if problems arise

### Fees

**Trading fees:**

* 0.1% base fee on all transactions
* Distributed: 50% to brokers, 30% to guilds, 20% to treasury
* Volume discounts for high-frequency traders
* Charitable exemptions for recovery trades

**Listing fees:**

* New ENFT listings require small fee
* Prevents spam and ensures quality
* Refunded if asset proves fraudulent
* Funds support market infrastructure

Available to all participants:

* **Prices**: Current bid/ask for all pairs
* **Volume**: Trading activity by pair and timeframe
* **Order book**: Depth of market at various prices
* **Recent trades**: Transaction history
* **Oracle data**: Physical world information

### Historical archives

Complete market history maintained:

* **Price charts**: All trading history preserved
* **Transaction records**: Full audit trails
* **Market events**: Significant occurrences documented
* **Analysis tools**: Public access to data for research

### Market indices

Tracking overall economic health:

* **BLEU Value Index (BVI)**: Broad economy measure
* **Sector indices**: Health, agriculture, energy, etc.
* **Regional indices**: Geographic economic performance
* **Virtue Index**: Ethical quality of trades

Markets connect to planetary finance:

* **Liquidity provision**: Vaults support markets
* **Stability**: Circuit breakers protect from shocks

### Governance oversight

Democratic control of markets:

* **Node Councils**: Approve local market rules
* **Tribunal**: Adjudicates disputes
* **Reparations Board**: Audits broker guilds
* **Phiyah Multisig**: Constitutional market changes
